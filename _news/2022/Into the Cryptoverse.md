### **Into the Cryptoverse**

<img src="/images/2022cryptoverse1.jpg" style="width:75%">

Catholic Junior College’s (CJC) Nexus Investment & Entrepreneurship Society (a co-curricular activity)  launched their inaugural Financial Literacy Week, ‘Into The Cryptoverse’, this week, 24-28 January. Through the week’s events, CJ Nexus hopes to educate CJCians on the opportunities and risks involved in the emerging financial technology industry.

<img src="/images/2022cryptoverse2.jpg" style="width:65%;margin-left:15px;" align = "right">

At the same time, CJCians will be introduced to the ‘Gallery of the Future’ where visitors can explore the metaverse using virtual reality headsets. Students are encouraged to sign up for limited spots via [CJ Nexus’ Instagram page](https://linktr.ee/Cj_Nexus).

<br clear="right">

<img src="/images/2022cryptoverse3.jpg" style="width:65%;margin-right:25px;" align = "left">

CJ Nexus has also collaborated with the business and finance societies of other junior colleges to learn more about business and finance programmes offered in the universities as well as explore future career opportunities.

<br clear="right">

Students keen to find out more can scan the QR code here or visit [CJ Nexus’ Instagram page](https://linktr.ee/Cj_Nexus).
