### **CJCares, CJ Celebrates online concert, and CJ Thanksgiving Mass**

<img src="/images/2021thanksgivingmass1.jpg" style="width:85%">

<b><u>CJCares Fundraising Project, 5 Aug 2021 to 31 Dec 2022</u></b><br>
As part of CJC’s culture of care for our students, the College will be launching the **CJCares Fundraising project** on 5 August 2021 to raise funds for CJCians who require financial assistance during these challenging times. Donations will go towards the CJC Needy Student Fund.  
  
We invite all CJCians, parents, staff, alumni and friends of CJC who wish to donate, to make a digital donation through the [giving.sg portal](http://giving.sg/catholic-junior-college) ([http://giving.sg/catholic-junior-college](http://giving.sg/catholic-junior-college)). Donations of $50 and above will be tax exempt.  Alternatively, donors may send a crossed cheque to Catholic Junior College at 129 Whitley Road, Singapore 297822. Please indicate donor's name, identification number, contact details (handphone or email) and “CJCares Fundraising Project” on the back of the cheque.  
  
The **CJCares** fundraising project will run from 5 August 2021 to 31 December 2022. We seek your generous support and contributions in Truth and Love as One Flame, One Family as we launch our **CJCares** fundraising project.

<b><u>CJ Celebrates online concert, 5 Aug</u></b><br>
To commemorate the launch of our CJCares Fundraising project, we will be holding our **CJ Celebrates Concert online** on **5 August 2021** from **5pm to 6.30pm.**  The virtual concert aims to showcase our students’ talents in the various performing arts CCAs as they celebrate their achievements in this year’s SYF 2021.  
  
Watch the concert here on [CJC's Youtube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZJufzDaXuc&list=PLY7_HyA5cSQZMQmF_QTXr5P5RR97buq5g).

<b><u>CJ Thanksgiving Mass, 4 Aug</u></b><br>
As a Catholic mission college, we will be organising an online **CJ Thanksgiving mass** on **4 August 2021,** from **5.30pm to 6.30pm** to thank God for his blessings on our College and the nation as we celebrate our nation’s 56th birthday and Catholic200SG.  

We invite all parents and staff to join us in our celebratory mass on 4 August to offer our thanks to God. The [zoom link](https://moe-singapore.zoom.us/j/82375173347) to the mass is shown below:
