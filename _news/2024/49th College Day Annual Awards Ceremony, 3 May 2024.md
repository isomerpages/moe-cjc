<h2><strong>49th College Day Annual Awards Ceremony, 3 May 2024</strong></h2>
<p></p>

<p>We are thrilled to share the highlights of our recent 49th College Day
event at Catholic Junior College. The occasion provided a wonderful opportunity
to honor and celebrate the remarkable achievements, unwavering efforts,
and exceptional resilience of our Class of 2023, as well as our present
cohorts.</p>

<p>Address by Guest of Honour, Dr. Radha Nagarajan, Class of 1980</p>
<p>The event was graced by the presence of Dr. Radha Nagarajan, Senior Vice
President and Chief Technology Officer of Optical Platforms at Marvell
Technology, and alumni from the Class of 1980. In his inspiring speech,
Dr. Radha encouraged our students to embrace curiosity and to always remember
the power of a smile, emphasizing the importance of these qualities in
their personal and professional journeys.</p>
<p></p>

<p>From academic excellence to outstanding contributions in co-curricular
activities, the event celebrated our students for their commitment to excellence
and their ability to overcome challenges. Their achievements serve as a
testament to their hard work, determination, and passion for learning.</p>

in shaping the academic and personal growth of our students.</p>
