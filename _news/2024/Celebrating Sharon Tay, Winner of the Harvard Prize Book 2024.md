<p>Join us in celebrating Sharon Tay, our winner of the Harvard Prize Book
2024! As the House Captain of Joseph House (2022/2023), Sharon was known
for her exceptional leadership and dedication to her peers. She displayed
remarkable determination and consistency, guiding and mentoring a team
of Sports Leaders while excelling in her academic pursuits. Sharon's genuine
concern for her peers' welfare was evident in her initiative to raise awareness
about mental health and her commitment to fostering inclusivity within
the school community.</p>
<p>In her personal statement, Sharon expressed her strong belief in driving
positive change within the college by advocating for the well-being of
her cohort and organizing events to create memorable college experiences.
Her commitment to giving back to the community through volunteering at
Bishan Community Club reflects her empathy and compassion for others. Sharon's
unwavering dedication to making a positive impact in the lives of those
around her is truly inspiring and deserving of this prestigious recognition.</p>
<p>Sharon's journey is a testament to her selfless acts and genuine empathy,
leaving an indelible impact on the school community and beyond. Her resilience,
leadership, and commitment to making a difference reflect her exceptional
character and make her a truly inspiring individual. Congratulations, Sharon,
on this well-deserved honor!</p>
