### **Thinker with a Mission, Leader with a Heart series 8**

<img src="/images/twm8.jpg" style="width:45%">

_By Nazra Shahnaz, 2T19, Class of 2021_

<b><u>Nazra Shahnaz, 2T19 - one of top five finalists of Halogen Foundation's National Young Leader Award 2021</u></b><br>
‘<i>Be&nbsp;the&nbsp;change&nbsp;you&nbsp;want&nbsp;to&nbsp;see</i>’&nbsp;\- I&nbsp;especially&nbsp;enjoy&nbsp;this quote&nbsp;because&nbsp;my&nbsp;purpose&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;leader&nbsp;is&nbsp;to&nbsp;embody&nbsp;it.

My name is Nazra Shahnaz, and I am a J2 student (2T19) from Catholic Junior College (CJC). I was nominated for the National Young Leader Award (NYLA) in March this year, and I never thought the 6-month journey would be one of the most life-changing experiences for me. It taught me how to carry myself as a leader, about the importance of communication, how to resolve a conflict in ideas and many more attributes I now hold close to my heart in being a leader.

My favourite phase of the award was the Leadership Trials. Though it was through a screen, I was able to meet many promising youth with such amazing ideas to bring to the table and this really amplified the faith I have in my peers. Through both the glory and shortfalls of my team, I was able to learn the importance of planning ahead, facilitating open communication, leveraging on the strengths of each team member to create a better outcome, and this shaped me to better prepare for the rest of my award journey and leadership opportunities to come. Meeting change-makers in a common place, finding middle ground with different inputs, and listening to the brilliant ideas everyone had helped me realise that I am not alone in my goals of creating positive change, and that leaders can better support one another through collaboration.

CJC&nbsp;has&nbsp;gifted&nbsp;me&nbsp;many&nbsp;leadership&nbsp;experiences;&nbsp;my&nbsp;teachers&nbsp;saw&nbsp;promise&nbsp;in&nbsp;me&nbsp;and&nbsp;wanted&nbsp;to&nbsp;help&nbsp;me develop&nbsp;it.&nbsp;My&nbsp;tenure&nbsp;as&nbsp;President&nbsp;of&nbsp;CJC&nbsp;Choir&nbsp;greatly&nbsp;shaped&nbsp;my&nbsp;leadership&nbsp;as&nbsp;navigating&nbsp;through&nbsp;the&nbsp;restrictions&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;volatile&nbsp;COVID-19&nbsp;situation&nbsp;left&nbsp;my&nbsp;team&nbsp;and I&nbsp;faced&nbsp;with&nbsp;many&nbsp;hurdles&nbsp;in&nbsp;planning&nbsp;and executing&nbsp;Choir&nbsp;sessions.&nbsp;Through&nbsp;the&nbsp;setbacks,&nbsp;we&nbsp;were&nbsp;able&nbsp;to&nbsp;learn&nbsp;to&nbsp;plan&nbsp;and&nbsp;brainstorm&nbsp;better&nbsp;and&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;leader,&nbsp;I learnt&nbsp;to&nbsp;communicate&nbsp;better.&nbsp;I&nbsp;found&nbsp;that&nbsp;one&nbsp;of&nbsp;a&nbsp;leader’s&nbsp;key&nbsp;purposes&nbsp;is&nbsp;to&nbsp;exude&nbsp;a&nbsp;spirit&nbsp;of&nbsp;enthusiasm&nbsp;and&nbsp;proactivity&nbsp;so&nbsp;as&nbsp;to&nbsp;keep&nbsp;morale&nbsp;and&nbsp;productivity&nbsp;up&nbsp;when&nbsp;times&nbsp;were&nbsp;tough.

When&nbsp;I&nbsp;founded&nbsp;[Shareducation](https://shareducation.webador.com/),&nbsp;my&nbsp;goal&nbsp;was&nbsp;to&nbsp;help&nbsp;bridge&nbsp;the&nbsp;gap&nbsp;between&nbsp;students&nbsp;who&nbsp;had&nbsp;a headstart&nbsp;in&nbsp;their&nbsp;studies&nbsp;due&nbsp;to&nbsp;having&nbsp;access&nbsp;to tuition&nbsp;services,&nbsp;and&nbsp;those&nbsp;who&nbsp;did&nbsp;not&nbsp;have&nbsp;that&nbsp;luxury.&nbsp;This&nbsp;came&nbsp;from&nbsp;a&nbsp;place&nbsp;of&nbsp;understanding&nbsp;as&nbsp;I&nbsp;too,&nbsp;found myself&nbsp;in&nbsp;a similar situation&nbsp;growing&nbsp;up.&nbsp;But&nbsp;after&nbsp;NYLA,&nbsp;I&nbsp;realised&nbsp;that&nbsp;my&nbsp;cause&nbsp;is&nbsp;so&nbsp;much&nbsp;more&nbsp;than&nbsp;that.&nbsp;I empower&nbsp;others&nbsp;by&nbsp;teaching&nbsp;my&nbsp;beneficiaries&nbsp;to&nbsp;create&nbsp;goals&nbsp;and&nbsp;aspirations,&nbsp;and&nbsp;that&nbsp;no&nbsp;matter&nbsp;how&nbsp;big&nbsp;or&nbsp;small they may seem,&nbsp;they are&nbsp;valid.&nbsp;I&nbsp;empower&nbsp;my&nbsp;team&nbsp;of&nbsp;30&nbsp;volunteers&nbsp;by&nbsp;allowing&nbsp;them&nbsp;to&nbsp;make&nbsp;full&nbsp;use&nbsp;of&nbsp;all&nbsp;the&nbsp;resources&nbsp;available&nbsp;to create&nbsp;a&nbsp;bigger&nbsp;impact&nbsp;and&nbsp;serve&nbsp;the&nbsp;community.&nbsp;

For&nbsp;as&nbsp;far&nbsp;as&nbsp;my&nbsp;mind&nbsp;can&nbsp;reach,&nbsp;NYLA,&nbsp;[Shareducation](https://shareducation.webador.com/),&nbsp;and&nbsp;my&nbsp;leadership&nbsp;experiences&nbsp;in&nbsp;CJC&nbsp;have&nbsp;only&nbsp;taught&nbsp;me about&nbsp;my&nbsp;true&nbsp;purpose:&nbsp;to&nbsp;not&nbsp;just&nbsp;be&nbsp;a&nbsp;leader,&nbsp;but&nbsp;a&nbsp;servant&nbsp;leader.&nbsp;To&nbsp;think&nbsp;with&nbsp;a&nbsp;mission,&nbsp;and&nbsp;lead&nbsp;with&nbsp;a&nbsp;heart.&nbsp;To&nbsp;have&nbsp;a&nbsp;zest&nbsp;for&nbsp;life&nbsp;and&nbsp;a&nbsp;generous&nbsp;spirit&nbsp;in&nbsp;my&nbsp;leadership.&nbsp;I&nbsp;see&nbsp;my&nbsp;potential&nbsp;to&nbsp;serve,&nbsp;and&nbsp;regardless&nbsp;of&nbsp;the profession&nbsp;I&nbsp;pursue&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;future,&nbsp;I&nbsp;would&nbsp;like&nbsp;to&nbsp;serve&nbsp;and&nbsp;change&nbsp;more&nbsp;lives.&nbsp;My&nbsp;hope&nbsp;for&nbsp;the&nbsp;future&nbsp;of&nbsp;youth&nbsp;is for&nbsp;more&nbsp;of&nbsp;us&nbsp;to&nbsp;find&nbsp;it&nbsp;in&nbsp;ourselves&nbsp;to&nbsp;step&nbsp;forward&nbsp;and&nbsp;build&nbsp;more&nbsp;inclusive&nbsp;societies.&nbsp;I&nbsp;am&nbsp;no&nbsp;perfect&nbsp;leader,&nbsp;but&nbsp;I&nbsp;am&nbsp;grateful&nbsp;that&nbsp;NYLA&nbsp;saw&nbsp;promise&nbsp;in&nbsp;me&nbsp;to&nbsp;do&nbsp;this.

Above&nbsp;all,&nbsp;having&nbsp;the&nbsp;honour&nbsp;to&nbsp;be&nbsp;a&nbsp;part&nbsp;of&nbsp;something&nbsp;as&nbsp;special&nbsp;as&nbsp;NYLA&nbsp;sparked&nbsp;a&nbsp;period&nbsp;of&nbsp;personal&nbsp;growth&nbsp;I never&nbsp;knew&nbsp;myself&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;people&nbsp;I&nbsp;work&nbsp;with&nbsp;needed&nbsp;—&nbsp;and&nbsp;beyond&nbsp;the&nbsp;cameras,&nbsp;interviews&nbsp;and&nbsp;blue&nbsp;pantsuits, I’m&nbsp;grateful&nbsp;for&nbsp;that.
