<p>To facilitate your enrollment into CJC, please fill in all relevant information
on CJ Portal, with the final step being to complete your subject registration
by <strong><u>3 Feb</u></strong><u> </u>before you report to CJC on 4 Feb&nbsp;.
Please visit the “Subject Combination Registration” tab on how to complete
your subject registration.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For questions related to subject combinations, you will have the opportunity
to raise them on 4 Feb. Rest assured, you are able to continue amending
your subject combination even after completing your subject registration
on 3 Feb.</p>
