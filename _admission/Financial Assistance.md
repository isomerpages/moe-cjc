<h3><strong>Financial Aid</strong></h3>
<p>The MOE FAS provides support to needy Singapore Citizen students, helping
them fully engage in school life and take advantage of academic, co-curricular,
and enrichment opportunities.</p>
<h4><strong>MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)</strong></h4>
<p>Eligibility</p>
<p>Students are eligible for MOE FAS if they meet either of the following
criteria:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Gross Monthly Household Income (GHI) of $4,000 or less, or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Per Capita Income (PCI) of $1,000 or less</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits for JC students:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Full subsidy of school fees ($6 per month)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Full subsidy of miscellaneous fees ($27 per month)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bursary of $1,600 per annum (inclusive of public transport subsidy)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Application</p>
<p>Applications can be submitted online at: <a href="https://form.gov.sg/68b7e92465cd36be287889c6" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://form.gov.sg/68b7e92465cd36be287889c6</a>
</p>
<p>There is no application cut-off date; students may apply at any time.</p>
<p>More information is available at: <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance</a>
</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>Income Family Micro-Insurance and Savings Scheme (IFMISS)</strong></h4>
<p>For students who are MOE FAS recipients, NTUC Income provides the<strong> </strong>Income
Family Micro-Insurance Scheme (IFMIS), which helps students cope financially
in the event of the death or total permanent disability of a parent or
guardian.</p>
<p>More details is available at:<strong> <a href="https://www.income.com.sg/orangeaid/ifmis" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.income.com.sg/orangeaid/ifmis</a></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>MOE Group Personal Accident (GPA) Insurance Plan</strong>
</p>
<p>All students are covered under the Ministry of Education Group Personal
Accident (GPA) Insurance Plan.</p>
<p>More information is available at: <a href="https://www.income.com.sg/group-insurance-for-schools-and-moe-personnel/group-personal-accident-for-students" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.income.com.sg/group-insurance-for-schools-and-moe-personnel/group-personal-accident-for-students</a>
</p>
<p>For any questions, please contact the College General Office<strong>.</strong>
</p>
